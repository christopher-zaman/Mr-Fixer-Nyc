    Our Story

    Founded in 2004, Mr Fixer NYC has been proudly serving New York City with reliable and professional handyman services. Starting as a small team, we earned certification and quickly expanded our expertise to include full-scale renovations. Over the years, we've become specialists in TV mounting and security camera installations, providing customized solutions to enhance the safety and comfort of your home or business.

    • Expert TV mounting for seamless entertainment setups
    • Professional security camera installations for peace of mind
    • Full home and business renovations tailored to your needs

    With a dedication to quality workmanship and customer satisfaction, Mr Fixer NYC is here to tackle projects of any size, ensuring every detail is handled with care.
